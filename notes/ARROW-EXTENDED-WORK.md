## 1. Headline finding — the out-of-ancestry sighting: modular / thermal time

The strongest new formalism is **Tomita–Takesaki modular flow / the
Connes–Rovelli thermal time hypothesis** (`gr-qc/9406019`). It is genuinely
out-of-ancestry (von Neumann algebras — not GR quotients, not finite-dim QI,
not classical probability, not Page–Wootters), and it fell out of the same-day
holographic-bound filing (`corpus/FRONTIER-2026.md` Front 4). The map onto the
hypothesis is not an analogy — it is the defining construction, and it
**reproduces the project's own clock-vs-arrow split**:

- **Self-containment ⇒ a canonical time (the clock half, ≈ P-A).** For a von
  Neumann algebra M with a **cyclic and separating** vector |Ω⟩ — the precise
  operator-algebra statement of "the state sees all of M and M annihilates
  nothing of it," i.e. mathematical self-containment — Tomita–Takesaki hands
  you a canonical one-parameter **modular automorphism group** σ_t generated by
  the state itself. Connes–Rovelli promote this to physics: time is the
  modular flow of the state, not a background parameter. σ_t is a *group* →
  reversible → a **clock, not an arrow**: exactly the P-A verdict, in a
  formalism that never appears in P-A's derivation. (Chua `2407.18948` is the
  one source that presses precisely this "is modular flow *directed*?" point —
  cite it as the skeptical check, not as a claim that modular flow alone gives
  a direction.)

- **The arrow needs the observer move ⇒ crossed product / GSL (the arrow half,
  ≈ P-B/S3).** A Type III₁ algebra (generic QFT / horizon) has modular flow but
  **no trace, no density matrix, no finite entropy** — no arrow to weigh.
  Witten's crossed product with the modular flow (`2112.12828`) turns it **Type
  II**, minting a trace and a finite von Neumann entropy equal to the
  generalized entropy S_gen = A/4G + S_matter (`2309.15897`). The **generalized
  second law** (Faulkner–Speranza `2405.00847`) is then the *monotonicity* of
  that entropy under algebra inclusion — a manufactured, irreversible arrow
  appearing exactly when the observer's structure is adjoined. This is the
  S3/P-B mechanism (conditioning → monotone entropy) rendered in operator
  algebras.

- **A second, independent de Sitter hit (strengthens S1).** CLPW,
  `2206.10780`: closing dS on a single static-patch observer *forces* a Type
  II₁ crossed product and a modular time. This is the **operator-algebra cousin
  of S1** — the same de Sitter closure phenomenon (single observer contains
  everything → temporal structure is what the closure costs/makes) in a
  *completely different formalism* from S1's GR quotient. Two formalisms, one
  phenomenon: this is the kind of cross-formalism convergence the verdict
  standard was written to reward.

### Independence assessment (tracker Rule 1 — stated honestly)

- The **clock half** (modular flow as intrinsic time from a cyclic-separating
  state) is genuinely independent of S1–S3. It *rhymes* with P-A, but P-A's
  clock is entanglement with an *external* register, whereas modular time is
  generated by the state's own algebra with **no outside** — more
  self-contained, not less. This is the novel content.
- The **arrow half** (GSL monotonicity) is only *partially* independent: it is
  a monotonicity-of-entropy-under-restriction statement — the same *species* as
  S3. Per Rule 1 ("sightings downstream of the same theorem count as ONE") I do
  **not** double-count it as fully new. Its value is the *unification*: it shows
  S3's classical-conditioning arrow and the field-theory GSL are one fact. One
  upgrade over my first read: the monotonicity here has **theorem** content
  (QNEC is proven in interacting QFT — Balakrishnan–Faulkner–Khandker–Wang
  `1706.09432`; Ceyhan–Faulkner `1812.04683` via half-sided modular inclusion),
  which is deeper than S3's elementary lemma, even if the same species.
- The **de Sitter hit** (CLPW) is the cleanest independent sighting: a distinct
  formalism reaching S1's exact conclusion.
- **Honesty flags:** the QFC (`1506.02669`) is a *conjecture* (only its QNEC
  limit is a theorem); the crossed-product = II construction is rigorous, but
  "modular flow = physical time" is a *hypothesis*, not a theorem; the GSL is
  proven only under Wall's assumptions (`1105.3445`), and Faulkner–Speranza is
  a semiclassical derivation. Tier the sighting **🟡 model-dependent**, not 🟢.

**Recommendation:** log **S4 = modular / thermal time** as a *qualified*
out-of-ancestry sighting (clock-half independent + a clean independent dS hit;
arrow-half unifies with S3 rather than adding). This meets the named upgrade
trigger — a fourth-formalism sighting — so move status from *"suggestive,
twice-tested"* to *"suggestive, twice-tested + one out-of-ancestry formalism
(qualified)."* Do not claim more. (S4 entry appended to the tracker.)

## 3. Buildable next steps for Track C (honest scope)

The operator-algebra content is **not** circuit-executable — do NOT claim
"modular flow on hardware" or "a gravitational algebra measured." Two things
ARE executable and stay in-idiom:

1. **(exact/sim) A finite-dim modular-flow toy — makes S4's split executable.**
   For a bipartite pure state with ρ_A = e^{−K_A}/Z (K_A the modular
   Hamiltonian), modular flow is e^{iK_A t} ρ e^{−iK_A t}. In ≤4 qubits show
   (a) modular flow is a reversible *group* (the clock, P-A analog) and (b) an
   inclusion/restriction gives a monotone *relative entropy* (the arrow,
   S3/GSL analog). This puts the clock-vs-arrow split in the repository's own
   idiom with zero gravitational overclaim, and it is a genuine test of S4's
   internal logic.

2. **(hardware, one free-tier window) Add a modular-flow control to
   `arrow_hardware.py`.** Today the lab has two families: ARROW (write records)
   and CONTROL (recordless). Add a third, MODULAR: apply the reversible modular
   group and show the system-alone entropy *returns* — a reversible clock line
   sitting between the monotone arrow and the flat noise floor. Slots straight
   into `circuit(t, records)`; ~16 more shallow circuits.

3. **(paper) Stake the Thread-7 gap.** If the literature truly lacks an
