# Tracker: the "reverse time" theme

**Purpose.** Time-reversal / temporal-orientation keeps surfacing uninvited
in this project. This document tracks each appearance with enough rigor to
eventually distinguish a genuine structural pattern from observer bias.
Started 2026-07-05, the day the theme appeared twice in one afternoon.

**Working hypothesis (falsifiable, stated before further data):**

> *Temporal orientation is the currency of self-containment.* Whenever a
> description of a system is forced to close on itself (an embedded
> observer, a self-referential encoding, a universe with no outside),
> the arrow of time is what gets manufactured, what cannot be reversed,
> or what loses global consistency — while spatial structure remains
> freely tradeable (buildable from entanglement, transportable,
> quantized).

**Logging rules (anti-numerology guardrails):**
1. Every sighting gets a tier (🟢 rigorous / 🟡 model-dependent /
   🔴 speculative) and an **independence assessment** — sightings that are
   downstream of the same theorem count as ONE.
2. **Misses are logged too**: places where the theme could have appeared
   and didn't. A pattern that never risks absence explains nothing.
3. Predictions derived from the hypothesis are logged before the
   experiments that test them.

### S2 — Holonomy census, det = +1 only (2026-07-05, Capstone VI)

Confirmed on hardware (job d958plnu62ks7396rgmg): frame rotation measured
at ~90% contrast, unrotated-frame overlap −0.003.
**Tier:** 🟢 (theorem + measurement).
**Independence:** partially "cheap" — ANY unitary framework excludes
antiunitary twists, so this sighting is guaranteed by quantum mechanics
itself. Its content: the exclusion is *generic to inside-operations*,
which is exactly what the hypothesis asserts. Future sightings that
reduce to Wigner's theorem count as S2, not as new.

### S3 — The conditional-entropy arrow (read 2026-07-05, in Hoffman,
Prakash & Prentner, *Fusions of Consciousness*, p. 24)

The arrow is manufactured by the act of viewing from within.
**Tier:** 🟢 (elementary information theory; the lemma, not the paper's
metaphysics). **Independence:** independent of S1/S2 (no quantum
mechanics, no relativity — pure probability).

### S0 — Standing background fact (whole project)

## Verdict standard

Three sightings across independent formalisms (GR quotients, quantum
information, classical probability) + one standing absence + zero
disqualifying misses = **suggestive; not yet evidence**. Upgrade
criteria: a P-prediction confirmed non-trivially, or a fourth
independent-formalism sighting. Downgrade criteria: a P-prediction
failing, or the misses column outgrowing the sightings.
